About me

Welcome! I am now a research scientist at Snap Research @ New York. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological University in Singapre in Feb 2025, supervised by Prof. Bihan Wen, and Prof. Alex Kot. Before that, I got my bachelor degree of computer science in 2020 from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC).

The central goal of my current research is to more effectively restore the visual world using either deep image priors (e.g., diffusion models, normalizing flows, GANs) or the degradation priors (e.g., noise formulations in the raw space)
